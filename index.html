About

Hello! I'm Sil, and I am a fashion futurist.

I overthink. Therefore, I imagine alternative futures where radical design, people, and tech collide.

I turn my fashion contradictions into speculative narratives designed to inspire reflection and curiosity on how we consume, perform, and relate to fashion.

Beneath the fun of learning new tools, playing with technology, and endless arguing, I like to think I take fashion seriously.
